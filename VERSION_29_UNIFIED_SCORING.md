# Version 29 — Unified Live Scoring Center

Version 29 reorganizes Live Scoring so the scorebox stays visible while every pitch and play is entered.

## Layout

- Team scores, inning, outs, bases, count, batter, pitcher, on-deck hitter, and in-the-hole hitter remain in one scoreboard.
- Ball, swinging strike, called strike, foul, and in-play buttons are reduced to approximately half the former height and placed inside the scorebox.
- Undo Pitch and Reset Count form the first action row.
- Record Error and Challenge form the second action row.
- Quick Result codes sit immediately beneath those action controls.
- The Challenge button reveals or hides the consolidated team ABS controls and challenge log.

## Palette

The scoring center now uses the approved Guariglia palette: deep brown, burnt orange, gold, cream, and paper white. The prior blue television-style colors were removed.

## Compatibility

All Version 28 scoring, substitutions, pitcher management, errors, ABS logic, continuous autosave, iPhone roster recovery, Excel export, and multi-page PDF behavior remain intact. Version 28 and Version 27.2 browser recovery data migrate automatically to Version 29.
