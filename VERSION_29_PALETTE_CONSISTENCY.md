# Version 29 — Palette Consistency Update

Version 29 now applies the approved Guariglia palette consistently across desktop, iPhone, ABS controls, the browser print layout, and generated PDFs.

## Approved palette

- Deep brown: `#3D2519`
- Burnt orange: `#9B4D1F`
- Dark burnt orange: `#733716`
- Gold: `#D9A441`
- Cream: `#FFF8E9`
- Warm cream: `#F6EAD0`
- Paper: `#FFFDF7`

## Changes

- Removed the remaining television-blue styling from the Live Game Center and ABS details.
- Recolored iPhone-native focus, radio, checkbox, selection, link, and tap states.
- Forced completed and blank PDFs to use the Guariglia palette for every matchup rather than switching to home-team colors.
- Updated the service-worker cache key so installed iPhone apps receive the corrected colors immediately after refresh/reopen.
- Retained all Version 29 scoring, saving, roster, substitution, error, ABS, Excel, and PDF behavior.
