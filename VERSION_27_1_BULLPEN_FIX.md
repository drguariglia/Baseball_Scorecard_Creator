# Version 27.1 controlling specification

Version 27.1 corrects the pitcher and reliever substitution menus.

- Loads each scheduled team’s active roster in addition to game-feed and probable-pitcher data.
- Places the probable starter first, then all available active pitchers and relievers.
- Provides up to 15 editable pitcher records per team: six scorecard rows plus nine bullpen rows.
- Removes a pitcher from the available dropdown after that pitcher has entered the game.
- Keeps previously used pitchers disabled in live scoring.
- Preserves six pitcher lines in Excel and PDF output, prioritizing the starter and pitchers who actually appeared.
- Retains all Version 27 game-ending, extra-inning, save/export, scoring, error, key-play, notes, and substitution behavior.
