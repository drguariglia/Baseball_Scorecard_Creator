# Version 31 — Plate Appearances / Current Scorecard Toggle

Version 31 adds a two-button live scoring view switcher. The Live Game Center remains visible while the scorer changes between:

- **Plate Appearances** — the existing editable plate-appearance grids.
- **Current Scorecard** — a live scorebook-style view with line score, batting grids, pitching order, totals, notes, and an automatic continuation page for innings 11–20.

The view selection is included in autosave and restored after a reload. Switching views does not modify the score, count, batting order, bases, substitutions, pitchers, errors, interference rulings, notes, or pitch history.

A dark horizontal line appears beneath the last batter of every completed half-inning in both views. The line is recalculated after edits, deletion, Undo Last Play, manual half-inning correction, saved-game restoration, and game-state rebuilding.
