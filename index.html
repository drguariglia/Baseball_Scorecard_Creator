ABS Challenges

Ball–Strike Challenges

Only the batter, pitcher, or catcher may challenge. The batter may challenge a called strike; the pitcher or catcher may challenge a called ball. The request must be immediate and cannot come from the manager, coaches, dugout, or another player. Successful challenges are retained; unsuccessful challenges are lost. A team entering an extra inning with none receives one challenge.

How to score: use Ball, Swinging Strike, Called Strike, Foul, and In Play to build the count. Ball four records a walk automatically. The pitch type on strike three determines swinging or looking strikeout; use K+ only when strike three was not legally caught. Template, recovery, transfer, and installation

Continuous recovery

Every field edit, pitch, play, substitution, challenge, note, and roster change is saved automatically in this browser. Team, lineup, bench, and pitcher information is also protected by a separate mobile recovery copy. Closing, refreshing, exporting, or moving the app to the background will not erase the live game.

Continuous autosave starting…

Save Game File remains the method for moving a game to another device. Browser autosave protects this device.

Blank scorecards

The completed-game PDF uses the selected home MLB team’s colors and clean computer scoring boxes. Blank PDFs let you choose whether to retain the traditional plus signs and 2×3 ball-strike guides.
