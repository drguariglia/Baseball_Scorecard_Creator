# Version 27.2 — Starter-First Add Pitcher Workflow

- The starting pitcher is the only pitcher shown initially for each team.
- A compact **Add Pitcher** dropdown appears directly beneath the visible pitcher list.
- Every unused active-roster pitcher remains available alphabetically by last name, then first name.
- Selecting a pitcher adds that pitcher to the visible list and makes the pitcher active.
- Previously selected pitchers remain visible in appearance order and are removed from the available dropdown.
- The entire bullpen is retained internally but is no longer displayed as a long editable list.
- **Add custom pitcher** preserves manual-game support with complete number, name, throwing hand, record, ERA, and strikeout fields.
- PDF and Excel exports continue to use the starter and pitchers who actually appeared.
