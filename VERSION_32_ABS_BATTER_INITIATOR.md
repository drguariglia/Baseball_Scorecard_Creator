# Version 32 — ABS Batter Initiator Correction

## Verified MLB rule

Under MLB's 2026 ABS Challenge System, only the batter, pitcher, or catcher may initiate a challenge. The batter may challenge a called strike for the batting team. The pitcher or catcher may challenge a called ball for the fielding team. The request must be made immediately after the pitch without assistance from the manager, coaches, dugout, or another player.

Official references:
- https://www.mlb.com/news/abs-challenge-system-mlb-2026
- https://www.mlb.com/interactive/mlb-abs-system-explainer
- https://baseballsavant.mlb.com/abs

## Application changes

- All three authorized initiators are always shown in the ABS dialog.
- Choosing Batter automatically selects the batting team and a called-strike challenge.
- Choosing Pitcher or Catcher automatically selects the fielding team and a called-ball challenge.
- The linked pitch is restricted to the correct call type for the selected initiator.
- Role changes immediately refresh the suggested player, assigned team, challenged call, explanatory rule text, and linked pitch.
- Existing ABS challenge counts, logs, autosave, game files, summaries, PDF/Excel notes, and extra-inning handling are preserved.

This is a maintenance update to Version 32; the version number is unchanged.
